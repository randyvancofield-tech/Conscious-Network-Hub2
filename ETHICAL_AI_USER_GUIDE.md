### 3. ‚ö†Ô∏è Report Issue (Issue Tracking Mode)

**What It Does**:
- Allows you to report platform bugs, suggest features, or raise concerns
- AI analyzes your issue and provides:
  - Acknowledgment and severity assessment
  - Recommended resolution steps
  - Priority level assignment
  - Support guidance

**How to Use**:
1. Click the **"Report Issue"** button at the top
2. Select the **Issue Category**:
   - Bug Report
   - Feature Request
   - Performance Issue
   - Usability Concern
   - Security Concern
   - Other
3. Enter a brief **Issue Title** (e.g., "Login page not responsive on mobile")
4. Provide detailed **Description** including:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Steps to reproduce (if it's a bug)
   - Expected behavior
5. Click **Submit Report**
6. Wait for AI analysis (usually instant)
7. You'll receive acknowledgment with severity, priority, and next steps
8. Can submit additional reports as needed

**Example Issue Reports**:
- "Dashboard loads slowly on certain pages"
- "Would like dark mode option"
- "Audio quality issues in provider sessions"
- "Confused about tier membership benefits"
- "Concerned about data privacy"

## Troubleshooting

### "Search layer temporarily offline"
- The service is experiencing a temporary connection issue
- Try refreshing the page or waiting a moment
- Come back to try again

### "Neural link lag detected"
- The AI is experiencing processing delays
- This is temporary; your question has been queued
- Try a simpler, more specific question

### Not getting the answer you need?
- Try rephrasing your question more specifically
- Select a different category to get different perspectives
- Report your concern using the Issue Reporting feature
- Contact support through the platform
